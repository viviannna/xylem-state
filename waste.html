Reduce. Reuse. Recycle.

Plastic production alone in 2015 gave rise to 3.8 percent of global carbon dioxide emissions while huge amounts of plastics lay unused and nondegradable in dumpsters. Plastic production has only been rising; recycling can help you significantly reduce your carbon footprint!

It Has Never Been Easier

Curbside Pickup

Get involved with recycling from your own front door! Many neighborhoods offer curbside recycling pickup, so all you have to do is collect your recycling in a separate bin and place it outside following the schedule. Check if your neighborhood offers curbside, and get started with a separate bin for recyclables.

Drop-Off Centers

Even if your neighborhood does not offer curbside pickup, there are other easy ways to recycle! Most communities have multiple recycling drop off centers - find a drop off center near you and periodically stop by to drop off your recycling waste. Begin by designating a separate bin for recyclables, and once it fills up take a trip to your local drop off center!
